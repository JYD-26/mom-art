The Artist

She painted
every season
for thirty years.

No fine arts degree. No gallery backing. Just a woman in Cheongju, Korea, who picked up a brush and never put it down. Over thirty years, she has shown work across Korea — from MBC M Gallery to juried competitions in Seoul — earning recognition in over a hundred exhibitions and awards.

Six solo exhibitions. Over 100 group shows. Competitions won across Chungbuk, Cheongju, and beyond — including the Chungbuk Art Exhibition, Dosol Art Prize, and the Fahrenheit Women's Art Award. These are not the paintings of a career. They are the paintings of a life.

Inquire About a Piece
"She didn't paint to sell. She painted because thirty years wasn't enough."

My mother is not famous. What she has is thirty years of canvases — each one a season of her life that I grew up watching her paint in Cheongju. Solo shows at MBC M Gallery. Exhibitions at the Darak Community Center. Group shows stretching from Korea to Japan.

Now I'm her daughter, and I'm sharing them with the world. Because these paintings deserve walls — not storage.

  • Solo Exhibitions — MBC M Gallery, Darak Community Art Center
  • Chungbuk Art Exhibition — 3× Selected
  • Dosol Art Prize · Fahrenheit Women's Art Award
  • Group Shows — Korea-Japan International Exhibition, Hyundai Fine Arts, Kim Bokjin Memorial Exhibition & more
  • Korea Fine Arts Association · Chungbuk Fine Arts Association
  • Cheongju Fine Arts Association · Daejeon Environmental Art Association
